# **Task 5: Stay Safe Online Checklist**





### **Prevention Checklist for Students**



* **Enable MFA:** 
* Always use Multi-Factor Authentication for your college email and social media accounts.
* 
* 
* **Verify Senders:** 

&#x20;  Check the actual email address of a sender before clicking any links, even if the name looks familiar.





* **UPI PIN Safety:** 

&#x20;  Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required to send or authorize a payment.





* **Check Recipient Name:** 

&#x20;  Always verify the name displayed in your UPI app before confirming a transaction.





* **Use Strong Passwords:** 

&#x20;  Avoid using predictable details like your date of birth or "123456" for your passwords.





* **Avoid Public Wi-Fi:** 

&#x20;  Never log into bank accounts or enter credit card details while connected to public, unsecured Wi-Fi networks.





* **Update Regularly:** 

&#x20;  Keep your phone's operating system and apps updated to ensure you have the latest security patches.





* **Think Before You Click:** 

&#x20;  If an offer or "emergency" message seems too good or too urgent to be true, it is likely a scam.





#### **Reporting Channels \* Portal**



* National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal at \[https://cybercrime.gov.in/]
* Helpline: Dial 1930 for 24x7 assistance.
